Use Cases

Power your applications

Fast, client-side PDF rendering and annotations for any use case

Document Signing

Enable digital signatures directly in your web application. Users can sign documents with ink signatures, stamps, or typed signatures.

Form Filling

Interactive form fields that users can fill out directly in the browser. Supports text inputs, checkboxes, radio buttons, and more.

Contract Review

Highlight and annotate contracts for legal review. Add comments, markups, and collaborate with team members in real-time.

Educational Materials

Display textbooks, course materials, and study guides. Students can highlight key passages and add notes for learning.

Invoice Processing

Review and annotate invoices directly in your accounting system. Mark up discrepancies and add approval notes.

Technical Documentation

Display API docs, user manuals, and technical specifications. Developers can annotate and share feedback on documentation.
